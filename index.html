Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a scam?

No, TrueClaim Services is a legitimate, licensed company. We operate under strict legal guidelines and maintain complete transparency throughout the process. Our success stories and professional credentials speak to our authenticity.

How much will this cost me?

We work on a contingency fee basis - meaning you don't pay unless we successfully recover your funds. Our fee is a percentage of the recovered amount, which will be clearly explained during our initial consultation.

How long does the process take?

The timeline varies depending on your specific case and jurisdiction. Typically, the process takes 2-4 months. We'll provide you with a more accurate timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific situation.
